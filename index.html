CMYK

A SMACSS/ SASS Demonstration Project

CYAN
Cyan is a greenish-blue color and is one of the three primary colours of the subtractive CMYK color model. It is located midway between blue and green, making it the complementary color of red.
MAGENTA
Magenta is a violet-red or purplish-red color, and is one of the three primary colours of the subtractive CMYK color model. It is located midway between blue and green, making it the complementary color of green.
YELLOW
Yellow is the color of gold, butter, ripe lemons, and the sun. In the spectrum of visible light, and in the traditional color wheel used by painters, yellow is found between green and orange.
BLACK
Black is the color of coal, ebony, and of outer space. It is the darkest color, the result of the absence of or complete absorption of light. It is the opposite of white and often represents darkness in contrast with light.
